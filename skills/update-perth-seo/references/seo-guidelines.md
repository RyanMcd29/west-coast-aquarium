# SEO Guidelines (Perth Small Business)

## Length targets

- Title: 50–60 characters (aim for <= 60).
- Description: 140–160 characters (aim for <= 160).

## Title patterns

- `Primary Service in Perth | Brand`
- `Brand | Primary Service in Perth, WA`
- Keep the primary keyword near the front.

## Description pattern

Short benefit statement + primary service + location + differentiator + CTA.

Example:
"Family-run aquarium servicing in Perth, WA. Fast maintenance, clear pricing, and friendly local technicians. Book a visit today."

## Local keyword patterns

- "Perth [service]"
- "[service] Perth WA"
- "[service] in Perth"
- "[service] near [suburb]" (only if page targets a suburb)

## Quality checks

- Unique per page.
- Matches on-page content.
- No clickbait or unverifiable claims.
- Open Graph/Twitter fields align with the page description.
